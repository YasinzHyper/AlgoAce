What it does

Personalised roadmaps

Goal + company + deadline → a week-by-week plan, auto-filled from 1,800+ company-tagged LeetCode problems.

AI feedback loop

Coaching that names what you solved and skipped, flags what’s at risk, and gives concrete focus areas.

Voice mock interviewer

Real-time voice technical, behavioural & system-design rounds — graded 0–100 against a rubric.

Timed challenges

Auto-built sets from your weak topics, difficulty-weighted scoring, and a live leaderboard.

Progress & analytics

Heatmap, streaks, topic mastery and pace-vs-plan — so you always know if you’re on track.

Instant explanations

On-demand AI walkthroughs — approach, steps and complexity — for any problem in the set.
